How The Internet Works
----------------------
- The internet is basically
    - A long piece of wire
    - A wire that connects different computers together in a global scale
    - Communicate & transfer data through the wire

    - There are computers on the wire that has a specific job
        - Available 24x7
        - Ready to serve users data & files
        - These computers are called "servers"

    - A computer used by a user is called a "client"
